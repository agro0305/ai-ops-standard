# AIS-0002 — Core Principles

1. Discover before acting.
2. Evidence over assumption.
3. Minimal change.
4. Backup before modification.
5. Explicit risk classification.
6. Human approval for high-risk actions.
7. Verify outcomes.
8. Rollback readiness.
9. Auditability.
10. Preserve existing systems.
