# AIS-0000 — AI-OPS Standard Introduction

AI-OPS is an open, vendor-neutral standard for safe and auditable AI operations on infrastructure and software systems.

## Problem statement

AI agents may assume tools are installed, choose occupied ports, overwrite configuration, omit backups, fail verification or provide no rollback.

## Goals

- safety
- repeatability
- auditability
- minimal change
- backup and rollback
- local-first operation
- vendor neutrality
- multi-agent interoperability
- MCP awareness
- compliance testing

## Non-goals

AI-OPS is not Ansible, Terraform, Kubernetes, Docker, an MCP server, an AI model or a replacement for deterministic safety logic.
