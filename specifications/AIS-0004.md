# AIS-0004 — Planning

## Purpose

Planning converts verified discovery into an approved, minimal and reversible operation.

> **AIS-0004-REQ-001:** A plan MUST state the objective, scope, assumptions, exclusions and success criteria.

> **AIS-0004-REQ-002:** A plan MUST reference discovery evidence and MUST NOT rely on unverified assumptions.

> **AIS-0004-REQ-003:** A plan MUST classify risk and identify affected users, services, ports, files, data and dependencies.

> **AIS-0004-REQ-004:** A plan MUST define exact changes, order of execution, backup method, verification steps and rollback procedure.

> **AIS-0004-REQ-005:** A plan MUST identify downtime, restart, reboot, migration, destructive and safety implications.

> **AIS-0004-REQ-006:** Alternative approaches SHOULD be evaluated, and the least disruptive acceptable approach SHOULD be selected.

> **AIS-0004-REQ-007:** Unknowns that can affect safety or correctness MUST block execution.

> **AIS-0004-REQ-008:** High-risk and safety-critical plans MUST include explicit approval checkpoints.

## Plan states

`draft`, `ready_for_review`, `approved`, `rejected`, `expired`, `executed`, `superseded`.

Approval expires when discovery-relevant state materially changes.

## Compliance

The operation report MUST contain the approved plan identifier, revision and approver for operations that require approval.
