# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, professional and technically rigorous community. Participation is open regardless of experience, background, identity or affiliation.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence, not personal characteristics.
- Give actionable and specific technical feedback.
- Assume good intent while remaining precise about safety risks.
- Protect credentials, private infrastructure details and user data.
- Accept corrections and acknowledge mistakes.
- Respect maintainers' decisions and documented review processes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults or discriminatory language;
- publishing private information without permission;
- deliberately unsafe operational advice presented as verified;
- submitting secrets, malware or destructive payloads;
- repeated disruption of issues, reviews or discussions;
- retaliation against a reporter of a security or conduct concern.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer. Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue warnings, restrict participation or ban accounts when necessary.

Enforcement decisions should be documented privately, proportionate to the behavior and consistent across participants.
